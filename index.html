Standard Curriculum

Kinder (3-6) Years

Children aged 3-6 should eat a balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and protein like eggs, meat, or beans. Offer healthy snacks and limit sugary foods. Ensure they stay hydrated with water or milk.

Elementary School

Children aged 6-10 need a balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and dairy for growth. Include healthy snacks like nuts or yogurt and limit junk food. Encourage drinking water or milk to stay hydrated.

Read More

Middle (10-16) Years

Children aged 11-16 need a nutrient-rich diet with fruits, vegetables, whole grains, lean proteins, and calcium-rich foods for growth and development. Limit sugary, processed foods and include healthy snacks like nuts or yogurt.
